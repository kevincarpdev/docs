## Content widgets

| Widget | Description | Common pages |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| **HeroSectionCarousel** | Full-width hero banner with multiple slides, CTAs, and background images | Homepage |
| **HeroVideoBlock** | Hero section with embedded video background | Landing pages |
| **TextBlock** | Simple rich text content block | Any page |
| **FullWidthBannerBlock** | Full-width promotional banner with image and CTA | Homepage, category pages |
| **SalesBannerBlock** | Promotional sale announcement banner | Homepage, sale pages |
| **MediaSection** | Combined media (image/video) with text content | About, info pages |
