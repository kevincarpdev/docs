## What you will manage

As a content creator, you will use **BigCommerce Page Builder** to manage the visual content on the Rust-Oleum HOME storefront. This includes:

- **Homepage** — Hero carousel, featured products, benefits blocks, how-it-works sections
- **Category pages** — Banners, FAQ sections, process blocks
- **Content pages** — Gallery, blog, DIY articles, FAQ, rewards, about/history
- **Product pages** — Page Builder widget regions above and below the main product view

## Accessing Page Builder

### Direct link

Open Page Builder directly:

[store-t5arr0qby1.mybigcommerce.com/manage/page-builder/?channelId=1](https://store-t5arr0qby1.mybigcommerce.com/manage/page-builder/?channelId=1)

### From BigCommerce admin

1. Log in to [store-t5arr0qby1.mybigcommerce.com/manage/dashboard](https://store-t5arr0qby1.mybigcommerce.com/manage/dashboard)
2. In the left sidebar, go to **Storefront > Pages**
3. Select a page and click **Edit in Page Builder**

Or navigate to **Storefront > Page Builder** and select a page from the dropdown at the top left.

## How content works

Content on the storefront is managed through **widgets** — reusable content blocks that you can drag onto pages and configure through a visual editor.

Each widget has:
- **A visual preview** — What you see on the page in the editor
- **Settings panel** — The left sidebar where you edit text, images, links, and other options
- **A specific purpose** — Hero banners, FAQ sections, image galleries, etc.

Your changes are saved to BigCommerce's servers and appear on the live storefront when you click **Publish**.

## What you need

- **BigCommerce admin access** with Page Builder permissions
- A modern web browser (Chrome recommended)
- Product images sized appropriately (see [Product images](/content/product-images))
